# Task: AWS Organization and Billing

## Goal

Set up the AWS Organization and billing ownership structure for the business.

## Context

- One shared AWS billing relationship is preferred.
- Internal admins are you and your partner.
- This is the foundation for all Bedrock usage.

## Scope

- Define the payer account and member account structure.
- Define who has admin access and how it is granted.
- Record the billing and governance model for the product.

## Acceptance Criteria

- The AWS billing owner is clearly defined.
- Internal admin access is documented and limited.
- The backlog reflects the single-subscription/shared-billing model.

## Open Questions

- Do we need separate AWS accounts for dev, staging, and production?
- Should the business entity be formed before or after the technical setup?

## Implementation Notes

- Keep this task focused on the organizational baseline, not app features.
