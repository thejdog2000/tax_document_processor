# Task: Staff Workflow Feedback

## Goal

Collect practical feedback from tax staff on what blocks adoption beyond the proof-of-concept.

## Context

The product must reduce mistakes first, speed up entry second, and look polished third.

## Scope

- Observe or simulate a staff member processing a packet.
- Identify confusing labels, missing states, and trust gaps.
- Capture where staff would still open the source PDF or manually check Excel.
- Prioritize fixes that reduce missed warnings and incorrect outputs.

## Acceptance Criteria

- At least five concrete staff-facing friction points are documented.
- Each friction point is tagged as mistake-prevention, speed, trust, or polish.
- Follow-up UI/backend tasks are added to the relevant epic.

## Suggested Questions

- What would make you trust or distrust the generated workbook?
- Which warning would you be most likely to miss?
- What would you still double-check manually?
- What slows you down during packet intake?
- What output would you expect to hand to a reviewer or preparer?
